# QRLife (qrlife.me) — Sponsor / Media Kit (One-Page)

## What QRLife is
**QRLife** is a secure QR + landing-page system that helps people share verified links and profiles safely.

Key idea: **scan → see the link first → choose what to do** (QRLife does *not* auto-open unknown links), which helps reduce QR scams and impostor/clone-profile risk.
