# Viking Voyage 2026 — Route Draft (weather-first, clockwise)

Start: **Mar 1, 2026** (Orlando → Mobile, AL first)
Style: RV **base camp** outside cities + flat-towed **Honda CR-V** day trips (≤ ~2 hours each way).
Preferences: state parks, state fairgrounds, Harvest Hosts/boondocking. Avoid RV driving in big cities.

## March 2026 (week-by-week)

### Week 1 (Mar 1–7): Orlando FL → Mobile AL area (Baldwin County base camp)
- RV transit: Orlando → Panhandle overnight → Baldwin County base
- Base areas: Spanish Fort / Daphne / Fairhope OR Gulf Shores / Fort Morgan (stay out of downtown Mobile)
- CR-V day trips (pick 2–4): USS Alabama, Fairhope, Dauphin Island, Fort Morgan/Gulf beaches, Pensacola (optional)

### Week 2 (Mar 8–14): Mobile area → Mississippi Gulf Coast
- Base areas: Ocean Springs / Gautier / Pascagoula side (reduce traffic)
- CR-V day trips: Ocean Springs, Gulf Islands NS, Biloxi (park once), Bay St. Louis, Ship Island (seasonal)

### Week 3 (Mar 15–21): Mississippi Coast → Louisiana (New Orleans by CR-V, not RV)
- Base areas: North Shore (Covington/Mandeville) OR west of New Orleans (parish parks)
- CR-V day trips: New Orleans (park once + transit/ride share), swamp tours (North Shore), Baton Rouge (long day), Lafayette (optional)

### Week 4 (Mar 22–31): Louisiana → Texas (Austin spring anchor: Mothership + Bambu)
- RV base areas outside Austin: Bastrop / Elgin / Lockhart / Taylor / Georgetown
- CR-V day trips: Austin (Mothership + Bambu), Lockhart BBQ, Hill Country (Fredericksburg/Wimberley), Gruene/New Braunfels, San Antonio (longer day)
